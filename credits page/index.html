About us

Kalvium is an educational organisation that is revolutionising how engineering degrees can be obtained. By focusing entirely on the practical work of being a software developer and ignoring the unnecessary topics, the programme greatly instils motivation and sheer interest in the mind of its students. From fun learning activities to coding a responsive website, Kalvium offers it all.
We, as students at Kalvium, have always provided great interest towards the programme from day one. Being presented with such colourful and frivolous opportunities in which one can secure his/her future has always been one of the major key stones of any student looking for a degree. The Kalvium programme provides you guaranteed internships from second year onwards which leads to an individual having no less than three years of work experience, before he’s finished his degree!
As a community, we here at Kalvium prioritise quality over quantity. To learn more about Kalvium’s functioning and student insights towards the programme, visit Kalvium’s official blog website :Kalvium Matters
